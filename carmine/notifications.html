    Notifications

    Notifications are reactive; they only appear when a user has completed an action.

    Item successfully deleted! ×

    Awesome, thanks for taking part! ×

    Whoops, we couldn't complete that action for you. ×

    Hmmm, it seems that item doesn't exist. × 

    <div class="notification">
  <p class="notification__message">
    Item successfully deleted!
    <a href="#" class="notification__close">×</a>
  </p>
</div>
<div class="notification notification--blue">
  <p class="notification__message">
    Awesome, thanks for taking part!
    <a href="#" class="notification__close">×</a>
  </p>
</div>
<div class="notification notification--yellow">
  <p class="notification__message">
    Whoops, we couldn't complete that action for you.
    <a href="#" class="notification__close">×</a>
  </p>
</div>
<div class="notification notification--red">
  <p class="notification__message">
    Hmmm, it seems that item doesn't exist.
    <a href="#" class="notification__close">×</a>
  </p>
</div>
