Colour Palette

GatherContent at it's heart is a platform for content-focussed collaboration, therefore we use colour sparingly across our app to avoid distracting our users.

Neutral

The use of the neutral palette throughout the app coupled with bursts of colour help catch the user's eye and draw attention to important actionables.
