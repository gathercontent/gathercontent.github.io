Notifications

Constant notifications

Constant notifications are used to inform users of an impending trial expiration, or for warnings that need to be actioned sooner rather than later. They span the full width of the page, ignoring any containers, and are usually placed at the top to be more visible to users.

Confirmation notifications

Confirmation notifications are used to inform users of changes to their content or otherwise, such as updating a template or deleting an item. They usually disappear off screen after a set period of time has elapsed.
