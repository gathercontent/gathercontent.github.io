    Brush types

    Closed brushes

    Closed brushes should be used for closed-path shapes, such as circles or squares.

    Closed brushes should never be used for lines, as they don't appear to be naturally drawn.

    Open brushes

    Open brushes should be be used for lines, as they appear to be naturally drawn.

    Open brushes should never be used for closed-path shapes, such as circles or squares.

    Open B brushes

    Open B brushes should never be used for closed-path shapes, as the lnes don't join naturally.

    Open B brushes should be used for lines that join onto a closed path shape, such as a circle or square.

    Notes

    • At smaller sizes (think 32px icons), some of these brushes may distort. In those cases, and for smaller details, don’t be afraid to use the basic brush to neaten up those lines.
    • For larger illustrations that need condensing down, remove detail from the smaller elements to retain clarity at smaller sizes.
