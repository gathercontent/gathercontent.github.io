    Secondary Palette

    Our secondary palette consists of 7 colours which are opacity percentages of the global palette. Illustrations should try to use the same opacity across colours to encourage consistency.

    Download

    The GC Colour Palette is available for download, and contains the 7 base colours along with their 90%-10% opacity colours.
